## YAML

Key-Value pair syntax:

```yaml
Fruit: Apple
Vegetable: Carrot
Liquid: Water
Meat: Chicken
```

Array/List syntax:

```yaml
Fruits:
	- Orange
	- Apple
	- Banana

Vegetables:
	- Carrot
	- Cauliflower
	- Brocolli
```

Dictionary/Map syntax:

```yaml
Banana:
	Calories: 1
	Fat: 2 g
	Carbs: 27 g

Grapes:
	Calories: 1
	Fat: 2 g
	Carbs: 3 g
```
